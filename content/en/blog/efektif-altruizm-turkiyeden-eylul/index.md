### ANNOUNCEMENTS📢

#### Beginning of the New Term for University Groups 🏫

The new academic term is well underway as one of the busiest and most exciting times of the year for many EA university groups. University groups provide an environment where many people learn about EA ideas and meet individuals motivated by the idea of doing good and taking important steps to tackle the world's most pressing problems. EA Boğaziçi, EA Koç and EA Bilkent also said hello to the new era.

#### EA Istanbul Meeting: Darren Mckee is Coming to Istanbul!

Darren McKee, author of the book *Uncontrollable*, which examines the risks that advanced artificial intelligence models may pose and how humanity can prepare for them, is coming to Istanbul. You are invited to the meetup on Tuesday, October 1 at 19:00 at [Vasalisa Çukurcuma](https://g.co/kgs/YPm4ADK).

#### Effective Altruism Hacettepe was established!

Effective Altruism (EA) is now at Hacettepe. [EA Hacettepe](https://www.instagram.com/eahacettepe/) aims to be a community of people who want to identify the world’s priority problems, develop the most effective solutions, and take responsibility for those solutions.

#### Future Impact Group's Research Program

The Oxford-based research group offers [a 12-week part-time research program](https://futureimpact.group) for researchers working on AI safety and policy. In this program, which includes a range of topics focused on solving the world’s most urgent problems, you can apply to the field of your choice and work with expert mentors to create global impact.

**🗓️ Application deadline: September 28**

#### AI Safety Turkey Campus Representatives Are Looking for!

To strengthen its connection with university students and academics interested in artificial intelligence in Turkey, AI Safety Turkey is [calling for Campus Representatives](https://forms.gle/HNEsibV7WDWQT34m9). Representatives will receive guidance for their career development and have the opportunity to connect with experts in the field of AI safety. They will also serve as AI Safety Turkey’s contact point at their university, organize events, and help expand the network. Open to all majors, this program requires less than 2 hours per week.

**🗓️ Application deadline: October 2, 2024.**

#### Effective Altruism Virtual Programs - EA Entry Program

In [this free, online 8-week program](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/virtual-programs/introductory-program), anyone from anywhere in the world can learn the basic principles of effective altruism and think deeply about how we can do the most good with the limited resources we have.

**🗓️ Application deadline: October 13**

#### Effective Altruism Virtual Programs - In-Depth EA Program

8-week event that anyone from all over the world can participate in [in this free and online program](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/virtual-programs/in-depth-program) You can delve deeper into effective altruism, reflect on complex questions such as ethics and human extinction risk, and learn about career opportunities in this field.

**🗓️ Application deadline: October 13**

#### Effective Altruism Virtuals Programs - Career Planning Program

[This is a 6 week program](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/virtual-programs/career-planning-program) Throughout, you can consider what a high-impact career means and learn how you can shape your career to contribute to solving the world's most pressing problems.

**🗓️ Application deadline: October 13**

#### Effective Altruism Virtual Programs - “The Precipice” Reading Group

The book The Precipice addresses the major risks facing humanity, such as nuclear weapons, climate change, artificial pandemics and out-of-control artificial intelligence. He argues that reducing these risks is one of the most important moral responsibilities of our time. It will take 8 weeks [to this online reading group](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/virtual-programs/the-precipice-reading-group) By participating, you can gain knowledge about protecting the future of humanity and learn how to deal with these dangers.

**🗓️ Application deadline: November 10**

[This database](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/yCZuFvbpTMZ7ot2BG/unjournal-research-with-potential-for-impact-database) It was created to ensure transparent evaluation and ranking of research with high impact potential in the field of applied and quantitative social sciences. #### EU AI Act and Turkish Artificial Intelligence Bill - AI Safety Turkey

It is thought that the EU AI Act, which aims to develop and use the European Union's artificial intelligence technologies in a safe, transparent and human rights-friendly manner, may have important consequences not only in Europe but also for everyone who develops and uses technology around the world. In Turkey, which follows EU regulations in many digital policies such as KVKK and e-commerce, an Artificial Intelligence Bill was presented to the parliament in June. To learn more about the EU AI Act, AI regulation efforts in Turkey and how the two differ [Written by Bengüsu Özcan](https://aisafetyturkiye.org/?p=110) You can check it out!

#### A Lead-Free Future: Finally On Its Way? | Center For Global Development

According to data from the World Health Organization, one in three children worldwide is exposed to dangerous levels of lead and more than 1.5 million people die due to lead poisoning every year. Although there is no data on deaths in Turkey, it is estimated that 6.3 million children have high levels of lead in their blood.

There was an important development last week against this silent health crisis, which is not on the radar of most governments, non-governmental organizations and philanthropists. Under the leadership of USAID and UNICEF, an international collaboration called "Partnership for a Lead-Free Future" (PLF) was launched. From the Center For Global Development, one of the important supporters of this initiative, which increased the amount of global funds allocated to combat lead poisoning from 15 million dollars to 150 million dollars. [Posted by Rachel Bonnifield](https://www.cgdev.org/blog/lead-free-future-finally-its-way) explains the importance and implications of this initiative.

#### What AI could mean for animals by Max Taylor — FAST Forum

While it is often discussed how rapid advances in artificial intelligence (AI) technology will affect the future of humanity, it is often overlooked how they may shape the fate of the trillions of animals with whom we share our planet. [This article by Max Taylor](https://forum.fastcommunity.org/posts/EGLymdxd5DYXReTzC/what-ai-could-mean-for-animals-1) examines how advanced AI models may affect animals across a wide range of areas, from livestock farming to animal testing.
