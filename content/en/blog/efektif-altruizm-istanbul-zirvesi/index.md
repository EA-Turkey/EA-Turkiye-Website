Applications are now open for Turkey's first Effective Altruism Summit!

- **Date:** October 18, 2025, Saturday, 11:00–19:00
- **Venue:** Kolektif House Maslak, Istanbul
- **Cost:** Free, open to everyone 18+

The program includes introductory and intermediate sessions, skill-building workshops, social events, and networking opportunities.
