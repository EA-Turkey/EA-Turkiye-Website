### **ANNOUNCEMENTS 🔊**

#### 🫂[EA Ankara Meeting](https://lu.ma/2lnh4vq6)

We are meeting face to face in Ankara on Friday, August 30th. If you are interested in Effective Altruism, whether you are a student or a professional, we would be delighted to have you with us. You can register [through this link](https://lu.ma/2lnh4vq6).

---

#### **📈[Impact Accelerator Program](https://www.highimpactprofessionals.org/impact-accelerator-program)**

This six-week online program aims to provide professionals who are not currently working in a highly cost-effective organization but who embrace the principles of Effective Altruism, with the tools necessary to make the world a better place.

🗓️ **Application deadline: September 5**

---

#### **👩🏼‍💻[Horizon Fellowship Cohort](https://horizonpublicservice.org/2025-horizon-fellowship-cohort-applications/?utm_source=Groups+Newsletter&utm_campaign=14c45b245e-EMAIL_CAMPAIGN_2022_01_12_05_48_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_7d52b2f96c-14c45b245e-319382205)**

The program offers 6 months of comprehensive training and financial support to individuals who aim to overcome policy challenges regarding artificial intelligence, biotechnology and other emerging technologies.

🗓️ **Application deadline: August 30**

#### **🐟[Aquatic Life Conference](https://www.ali.fish/alc24)**

This conference, to be held online September 9-10, will host important discussions and presentations highlighting efforts to protect marine life and build a more sustainable, ethical future in seafood production.

**🗓️ Application deadline: September 8**

#### **[Building Your Alt Protein Career](https://gfi.org/event/ask-gfi-alt-protein-careers-9-10-2024/)**

In this online event, which will be organized by the Good Food Institute on September 10, comprehensive information will be given about the academic and industrial opportunities offered by this field for those considering a career in the alternative protein sector, and participants' questions will be answered.

**🗓️ Application deadline: September 9**

#### **[EAGx Berlin Conference](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/eagxberlin-2024)**

Between 13-15 September **[This conference will be held in Berlin](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/eagxberlin-2024)** By participating, you can connect with effective altruism communities around the world, attend various workshops and talks, have the opportunity to chat with people with similar interests or career paths, and deepen your connection to Effective Altruism.

**🗓️ Application deadline: August 28**

#### **[Call for Proposals for High Impact Mental Health Projects](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/fdjyAhYuc3oYfdZuk/rfp-for-high-impact-mental-health-projects)**

#### “[Why We Shut Down](https://asteriskmag.com/issues/07/why-we-shut-down)”

“More than $200 billion is spent on development programs each year, but assessing whether the program is working – and stopping programs when resources could be better used elsewhere – remains the exception,” Sarah Eustis-Guthrie and Ben Williamson, founders of the Maternal Health Initiative, describe their experience shutting down their organization after their pilot program had far less impact than they expected.
