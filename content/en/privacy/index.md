This notice explains how Effective Altruism Turkey ("EA Türkiye"), acting as data controller under the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK"), processes your personal data when you use the contact form on eaturkiye.org.

## What data do we collect?

When you submit the contact form, only the information in the form is processed:

- First and last name
- Email address
- Organization (optional)
- City (optional)
- Selected area of interest
- Your message
- The language you used, the page the form was submitted from, and your consent choice

The site collects no other form data — there are no accounts, memberships, or tracking forms.

## Why and on what legal basis do we process it?

Your data is processed so that we can reply to your message and follow up on your request, based on our legitimate interest under KVKK art. 5/2(f) and on your explicit consent. The checkbox on the form records your consent to storing your contact details for this purpose.

## Where is it stored and who receives it?

- Form submissions are stored in our infrastructure provider Cloudflare's D1 database.
- When a new submission arrives, a copy of the message is delivered via the email notification service Resend (based in the US) to bilgi@eaturkiye.org only. This constitutes a transfer of your data abroad.
- To block spam, the form is verified with Cloudflare Turnstile; Cloudflare may process technical verification data in the process.

Your data is not shared with any other third party and is never used for advertising or profiling.

## How long do we keep it?

Submissions are kept for as long as needed to answer your request and for a reasonable follow-up period; records that no longer serve that purpose are deleted periodically.

## Your rights under KVKK

Under KVKK art. 11 you have the right to access your data, request its correction or deletion, object to its processing, and learn which third parties it has been transferred to. To exercise these rights, write to [bilgi@eaturkiye.org](mailto:bilgi@eaturkiye.org).
