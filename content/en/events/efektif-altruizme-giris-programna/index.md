We invite you to the 5-week free reading group, where we will seek answers to the question of how to solve the world's most urgent problems in the most effective way!

This program, which will be carried out in groups of 5-8 people, will last five weeks and take place online. Every week, we will read selected texts and discuss them among ourselves with a moderator. The readings of the program, which will start in the last week of July, will be in English, but the sessions/discussions will be in Turkish.

Whether you are a student or a working professional, if effective altruism interests you, you can [apply to this program](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSefNlWCMTaTTh2kPArfFzwo03DNPziHQadYWaqD4_j9dNMthg/viewform?usp=header) regardless of where you live or your intellectual background. The application deadline is July 22.

For details, you can join the info session or our WhatsApp group.

• [WhatsApp Group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/Fcw9tNIoyfCL9eBdLrDKYL)

• [Online info session](https://lu.ma/u5vpyrrn) - Sunday, 20 July, 19.00
