[Effective Animal Advocacy Turkey](https://www.instagram.com/effectiveanimaladvocacytr?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==) (EAATR) is a community for people who want to create the most effective change for animals. Its aim is to strengthen advocacy through evidence-based and strategic approaches, and to support animal advocates in improving their knowledge, skills, and connections through reading groups, trainings, workshops, and content.

Anyone who wants a better world for animals, values impact-oriented approaches, or wants to get to know this community is invited to their meeting on Sunday, September 14!

🗓 When? Sunday, September 14 – 17.00

📍 Where? Türk-Alman Kitabevi, Beyoğlu / İstanbul
