The Effective Altruism Introduction Program is a free 5-week program for those who want to explore the most effective ways to help others.

The program starts the week of July 6. Participants are expected to commit around 3 hours per week: 1.5 hours of English readings + 1.5 hours of Turkish-language discussion sessions.

**Topics covered:**
- Effective thinking and what is effective altruism?
- Animal welfare
- Long-term future potential
- AI and biotechnology risks
- Career impact

**Who can apply?**
Anyone 18 or older. Priority is given to university students without an EA community on campus and working or job-seeking professionals. A minimum B2 English reading level is required. The program is free.
