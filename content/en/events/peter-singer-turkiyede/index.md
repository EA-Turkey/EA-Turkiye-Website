Peter Singer, one of the founders of effective altruism, is giving a talk **on Wednesday, May 5 at 12.30** at the invitation of Bilkent University Department of Philosophy. The event will be held on the department's [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCzij4ZE6X-bu1d9t6DJhlLA).

Singer's book *The Life You Can Save* will soon be available in Turkish under the title *Kurtarabileceğin Hayat*!
