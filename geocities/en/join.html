MINI FAQ

What should someone new do first?

Looking for an intro event, sending a short e-mail, or reading a bit to orient yourself are all solid starting points.

Which cities are active?

Istanbul and Ankara are the clearest current hubs, while online connection still matters too.

Do I need technical expertise?

No. Curiosity and good faith are more important than any particular professional background.
